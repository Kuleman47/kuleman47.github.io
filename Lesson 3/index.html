Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to keep people up to date on the weather in their local area. The user can also look up the weather for other areas as well so if they were planning on taking a trip to the beach they can look up the weather there before they arrive at their destination. they can see the next 24 hours and up to 10 days from the time the user is looking at the weather.
