Target Audience

The Target Audience for this website is one of all ages 10+

Personas

It is safe for kids to come and look at what the weather is for their area or for other areas if they need it. It is also for anyone who wants to know the weather in their area so if they would like to go on a walk or bike ride or anything they are able to and won't need to worry about what the weather is going to look like.

Scenarios

  1. Is it cold outside?
  2. Is it raining?
  3. Is it going to snow this week?
  4. What is the high for the day?
  5. What is the weather in Los Angeles California?
